- Gotta Catch 'Em All -

The Pokémon series follows young Trainers as they embark on a journey to become Pokémon Masters. Starting with a Pokémon partner from a local Professor, they travel across various regions, battling Gym Leaders to earn Badges, thwarting villainous teams with nefarious plans, and capturing diverse Pokémon to complete their Pokédex. Along the way, they encounter and sometimes calm or capture Legendary and Mythical Pokémon, culminating in a challenge against the Pokémon League's Elite Four and the reigning Champion. The series emphasizes themes of friendship, growth, and respect for nature, encapsulated in the quest to be the very best.
